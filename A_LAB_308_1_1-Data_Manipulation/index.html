Road Trip Planning Scenario

ALAB 308.1.1 - Data Manipulation with JavaScript

Let’s look at a more practical scenario. You are planning a cross-country road trip!

  • Total Distance: 1,500 miles
  • Fuel Budget: $175
  • Average Fuel Cost: $3 per gallon

Fuel Efficiency breakdown:

  • At 55 mph: 30 miles per gallon
  • At 60 mph: 28 miles per gallon
  • At 75 mph: 23 miles per gallon

Program Requirements:
Set up a program to answer the following questions:
1. How many gallons of fuel will you need for the entire trip?
2. Will your budget be enough to cover the fuel expense?
3. How long will the trip take, in hours?
4. Compare results at 55, 60, and 75 mph. Which makes the most sense?

Log the results of your calculations using string concatenation or template literals.
